## AI-assisted approach

If you use an AI coding tool, you can ask it to review this site and to add the items you need.

## Manual approach 

1. Find the list of glossary entries in: `_data/glossary.csv`

2. Edit or add entries as required - making sure that the definitions are surrounded by quotes

The first time a glossary entry appears on a page (outside of a heading), it will be highlighted with a link. Users can click on it to open the definition.
